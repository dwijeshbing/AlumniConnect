Anand Illa

Mr. Anand Illa began his career with a degree in VLSI Design from IIT Madras, graduating in 2003. He joined Intel Corporation, where he has held various roles over the years. Starting as a Design Automation Engineer, he progressed through positions including Senior Design Engineer, Engineering Manager, and Director, ultimately becoming a Sr. Director in SOC Design. In these roles, he led teams responsible for SOC SD execution, physical integration, and design methodologies for high-profile products. In addition to his work at Intel, he has been self-employed since 2020, analyzing Agritech technologies with a focus on AI/ML solutions and making micro investments in fintech startups based in Bangalore. His extensive experience in semiconductor design and his entrepreneurial ventures highlight his expertise in technology and innovation.
